The Sims 4

Aspire Challenge

In the Aspire challenge, players must complete a new aspiration with each new generation with the goal of completing as many aspirations as possible. Sims can also only earn money doing careers or other activities related to their aspirations. I have provided some suggestions of ways to earn money for each aspiration, but feel free to use your imagination as well and apply the rules however you see fit!

Along with completing aspirations, some secondary tasks have also been included in the challenge. These tasks are not mandatory, but are intended to add an extra challenge to those interested. They will also add to the total point score of the challenge.

Setup

Create your first sim: design them however you want, give them any traits you'd like and select your first aspiration from the options below. You can have as many sims/pets as you want in your starter family, but each member is still only allowed to make money doing things related to their aspiration. You may select all the traits and aspirations for everyone in your starter family, but once you exit CAS you may not change these without a trait changing potion or by other in-game means.
